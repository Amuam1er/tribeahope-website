About Trinity Beacon of Hope

⚖️

Our Mission

Trinity Beacon of Hope (TRIBEHOPE) is a non-governmental, non-denominational, non-tribal, and non-political association dedicated to serving the social, economic, and civic needs of communities across Cameroon.

Our Values

Faith • Hope • Health • Development • Peace • Justice

Established

Founded in 2022 and officially registered in 2023, TRIBEHOPE has been actively providing voluntary assistance to vulnerable populations throughout Cameroon, with our headquarters in Limbe, South West Region.

Our Symbol

Our logo represents the Symbol of Balance and closed hands to symbolize peace, reflecting our commitment to justice and unity in our communities.

Our Services

🛡️

Gender-Based Violence Support

Comprehensive support services for survivors of gender-based violence, including counseling, legal assistance, and safe shelter referrals.

👩‍⚖️

Women's Rights Advocacy

Fighting discrimination against women and girls, promoting gender equality in civil, traditional, business, and political domains.

💡

Skills Empowerment Training

Providing gainful skills training and self-sufficiency projects to empower women, youth, and vulnerable populations.

🏥

Health & Wellness

Health interventions, awareness campaigns, and assistance for disabled persons and children in need of medical care.

👴

Elderly Care Services

Promoting the rights and well-being of the elderly, fighting against abandonment, and establishing care facilities.

🌍

Environmental Conservation

Promoting nature conservation, sustainable agriculture, and environmental awareness in rural and urban communities.

Recent Activities & News

Gender-Based Violence Awareness Campaign in Buea

TRIBEHOPE successfully conducted a three-day awareness campaign in Buea, reaching over 500 community members with information about preventing gender-based violence and supporting survivors.

🤝

Partnership Signed with FIDA Cameroon

TRIBEHOPE formalized a strategic partnership with FIDA Cameroon to enhance legal support services for women and strengthen advocacy efforts across the South West Region.

💼
August 28, 2025

Skills Training Program Graduates 45 Women

Our vocational training program in tailoring and small business management celebrated its latest graduation of 45 women from various communities in Fako Division.

🏥
August 20, 2025

Medical Outreach in Rural Villages

TRIBEHOPE organized a medical outreach program in remote villages, providing free health screenings and medications to over 300 elderly residents and children.

🎓
August 10, 2025

Educational Workshop on Women's Rights

In collaboration with the Ministry of Women Affairs, we conducted an educational workshop on women's legal rights, attended by 200 women from across the South West Region.

🌱

Environmental Conservation Project Launched

TRIBEHOPE launched a tree planting initiative in collaboration with local communities, aiming to plant 1,000 trees across deforested areas in the South West Region.

Family Reunification Program Success

Our displacement assistance program successfully reunited 25 families who were separated due to ongoing conflicts in the North West and South West regions.

With support from specialized legal experts, TRIBEHOPE opened its first legal aid clinic in Limbe, offering free legal consultation services to vulnerable community members.
